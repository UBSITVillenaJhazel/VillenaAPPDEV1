Diaries during Christmas

Christmas day. I enjoyed the day telling stories with my beautiful cousins. I also volunteered to facilitate the program. It was wonderful to be celebrating together not only with a family but the whole clan.

Barangay Christmas & New Year Party. I was a part of SK Officials and Women's Organozation. In addition, I am a youth volunteer so I have two celebrations at once but it was kinda tiring.

New Year's Eve. Hahaha. Every dark side of the sky has exploded fireworks where it makes things beautitful at night. We shouted at the top of our lungs "Happy New Year".
